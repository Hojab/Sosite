Bio

Welcome to my website! I’m Sonia Ahrabi Asli, a sculptress, bas-relief artist, ceramist, and more. In addition to my YouTube channel, I have over 30 years of experience in creating sculptures, pottery, and ceramics, and have crafted numerous works over the years. Here, I will share my artistic journey, along with tips and insights for fellow artists. Whether you’re an art enthusiast or simply curious, I invite you to explore and join me in this creative adventure! Don’t forget to subscribe to my YouTube channel for more artistic inspiration!

What I Can Do

Sculpting

Sculpting is a form of creating three-dimensional art using materials such as clay, stone, or metal. My work as a sculptress focuses on expressive, detailed figures that capture human emotion and movement.

Bas-Relief

Bas-relief is a type of sculpture in which the figures project slightly from a flat background. My bas-reliefs capture moments in time with intricate detailing, often depicting nature, people, and abstract designs.

Pottery

Pottery is the art of shaping clay into functional and decorative items. I specialize in creating beautiful, handmade ceramic pieces that combine both artistry and utility.
